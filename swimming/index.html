Swimming

I swim on a very regular basis. I swam competitively as a kid and got back into it for exercise around the end of 2012. Since my work and personal travels take me to interesting places around the world, tracking down a place to swim has become a fun challenge. I have swam in places like mountain lakes, tropical lagoons, and the world's northern most swimming pool. And, as an information management officer, I, of course, keep track of all those locations!
